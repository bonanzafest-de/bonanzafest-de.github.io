Bonanzafest 2024

‍

This year's Bonanzafest took place on July 13 and 14 in Cologne's Südstadt district, once again at the Altes Pfandhaus. Like last year, it was a stimulating mix of performance and music, visual and artistic impressions in the form of films and exhibitions, workshops and, of course, a party and our community mixer.
