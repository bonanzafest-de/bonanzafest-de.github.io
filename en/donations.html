Donations

Bonanzafest is a non-profit festival financed by public funding. We want to pay the artists appropriately, equipment has to be rented and organisational structures should be made sustainable. We try to do all this with the support of our supporters and sponsors. However, Bonanzafest is still growing and many internal budgets are tight. We wholeheartedly believe in Bonanzafest as a celebration of art, queerness and community. Your support, no matter the size, will help us bring our vision of Bonanzafest to life.
