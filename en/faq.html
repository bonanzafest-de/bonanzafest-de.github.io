How much does the Bonanzafest cost?

For the programme of the Bonanzafest 2024 there is a festival pass which is valid for the whole weekend. There are the following categories:
FESTIVALPASS SOLI for 55 euros
FESTIVALPASS REGULAR for 35 Euro
FESTIVALPASS COMMUNITY for 15 euros
You can buy your ticket in the Ticketshop.

Can I get into all the events with my festival pass?

For this year's Bonanzafest programme, there is a festival pass that is valid for the whole weekend. With this pass you can visit the exhibition, the performances, the lounge and the community spaces. only if you want to participate in a workshop please use the registration.

Can I book tickets for individual events?

In principle, you gain access to the Bonanzafest by purchasing the festival pass.

What can I do if I can't afford a festival pass?

It is possible to take on two shifts as a volunteer, for which you will receive a festival pass. f this is not possible for you, please send us an email and we will find a solution together.

Can I still buy a festival pass on the spot?

Our admission team will be available to help you buy your pass on site  via our Ticketshop. There will be no cash desk and no individual tickets.

My preferred workshop is fully booked, how do I get on the waiting list?

If the places are fully booked, you can put your name on a waiting list (kontakt@bonanzafest.de, subject: waiting list). We also encourage you to just show up on the day 10 to 5 minutes before the workshop at the Infopoint to check out if spots have opened up.

Is the Bonanzafest barrier-free?

There are stairs throughout the house. The important entrances are accessible with ramps. Unfortunately, to get to the lower exhibition space, you need to be accompanied by a person from the awareness team, as the exit is relatively steep and goes through the supply wing of the Altes Pfandhaus. The building has toilets in the basement, which are not wheelchair accessible. There is also a wheelchair accessible toilet in the courtyard. You can find more information under "Barriers".

Are there rest rooms?

If things get too much for you, the awareness team will show you a place to retreat with water, tissues and comfortable seating. There is also a BIPoC retreat room. Also check under "Barriers".

Is there an awareness team on site and how do I recognise them?

For us, part of a great event is that everyone feels comfortable and that we create a space that is as discrimination-free as possible. You can read more information about awareness under " Awareness".

What should I bring to the Community Brunch?

The Community Brunch will take place on Sunday mornings from 11 am to 1 pm. We would be happy if you contributed something to the buffet. You are free to choose whether sweet or savoury, but please everything vegan or vegetarian. Please make sure to list all allergens (wheat, eggs, nuts, etc.) on a piece of paper. Thank you!

What is the pricing policy for drinks?

At the bar we also follow the solidarity approach, here it's "pay what you want/can". A price range is recommended, so it's up to you how much you give.
