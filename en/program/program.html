Program 2025

This year's festival edition will take place between the 22nd and 29th of November as an exhibition with performances at Bunker K101 in Köln-Ehrenfeld.

Under the motto Respectability Won't Save Us - Queer Intimacies and Companionships as Realities for Change, we are organizing a diverse program of exhibitions, interactive performances, and workshops in collaboration with the sw_of_cgn (Köln), Bendix Mignon (Hamburg), Kumi More (Hamburg), Jasper J. Maurer (Berlin), and The Combination (Berlin, Leipzig, Köln).
